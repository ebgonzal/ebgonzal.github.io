Biodiversity loss compensation

I am working on the implementation of a Biodiversity Loss Compensation Tool (BLCT) to ensure that construction or industrial projects consider the conservation and care of biodiversity, generating a net gain within the framework of the environmental impact assessment procedure. Together with researchers, academics, and stakeholders involved in biodiversity conservation, we have designed a guide outlining guidelines and strategies for the allocation of BLCT. My role as the leader of the GIS and remote sensing team involved the development of a map of the Buenos Aires province that quantifies ecological importance. Leaflet and Chart.JS.
